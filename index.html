The Concept Studio Launches

April 22, 2015

The Concept Studio (TCS), a digital marketing agency, announces the launch of TCS Digital Media, a new digital media planning and buying service delivering closed loop, data driven ad marketing solutions that are cross channel, leveraging hyper-local data and analytics for its clients.

“We’ve teamed up with Digital Mouth, a Charlotte, North Carolina based digital media agency to offer our clients the most innovative ways to reach their target audience,” said Stephen O’Shea, President and Founder of The Concept Studio, “We know data is king—and with those insights we can reach consumers across mobile, tablet and desktop - a robust set of premiere digital ad networks and publishers with the latest in technology and personalized content, while tracking the performance in real time for on-the-fly campaign optimization and retargeting marketing campaigns. This approach and solution that spans Social, Display and SEO delivers attribution, data and analytics at scale and an unprecedented level of marketing flexibility and ROI accountability.”

Digital Mouth for TCS means a satellite office in Charlotte and an expanded team that will provide digital media strategy, planning for integrated programs with cross-channel delivery, effective audience targeting and combine them with TCS’s proven track record delivering ‘Ideas That Make A Difference’ through creative and marketing program execution. This is a powerful addition to TCS’s core set of capabilities that enables its clients to now have an end-to-end digital marketing campaign and solution delivering ultra-efficiency and effectiveness.

Another key feature to the TCS Digital Media offering is the mobile-specific ad platform. As mobile devices continue to shape our lives, search behavior and buying habits, the TCS mobile media platform helps reinvent client promotions and coupons through mobile for greater reach, personalization, redemption and ROI.

“This is an exciting time at TCS,” O’Shea said, “As the agency’s business continues to grow and our world pushes forward with its rapid adoption of technology and morphs into a mobile and social centric society, we’re focused on bringing forth the next generation of marketing and advertising services. Additional new partnerships, products and services are set to be announced in the coming months.”
